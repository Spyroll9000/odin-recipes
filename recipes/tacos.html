Easy Family Tacos

Easy - 30 Minute Family Tacos

These tacos will be the easiest and tastiest dinner in your household.

Super simple - only takes 30 minutes. Are you ready?

Click here for a 30 minute timer


    Ingredients

  • 1lb beef (80% or higher)*
  • 1 container of sour cream
  • 1lb bag of mexican cheese blend*
  • 1 can black olives (cut - pitted)
  • 1 pre-made package of guacamole
  • 1 package of tortillas*
  • 1 jar of salsa
  • 1 bag of Romaine lettuce
  • 2 roma tomatoes
  • 2 jalapeños
  • 1 yellow onion*

The ones with * next to them are mandatory.
If you are unable to get certain ingredients, do not worry.
The tacos will be delicious without them.

    Preparation Steps

  1. Wash jalapeños and roma tomato in lukewarm water.
  2. Dice onions into 1/4 inch chunks
  3. Trim ends of jalapeños, cut pepper in half lengthwise, remove white membrane and seeds, lay halves flat on cutting board and cut into strips. Rotate the strips and cut crosswise.
  4. Cut the ends of the tomato off and place tomato (inside down) on the cutting board. Slice all the way through lengthwise, rotate and cut crosswise to create small squares.

    Cooking Steps

  1. Heat oil in a medium frying pan on medium heat until you see small bubbles in the oil (but not enough that it's popping) and put in the onions and jalapeños.
  2. Stir frequently until onions are a transparent color and jalapeños have darkened. This may take up to 10 minutes depending on temperature of oil.
  3. Season with a tablespoon of paprika, a tablespoon of garlic powder, a teaspoon of salt, a teaspoon of Tony's, a splash of Worcestershire sauce, and fresh cracked pepper.
  4. Remove from pan and add beef into the same pan and brown it on medium high heat.
  5. Once it is about 80% browned, add the same seasoning as the vegetables had and then mix well. Add vegtables afterwards.
  6. Add a packet of any taco season for flavor and follow instructions on packet for how much water and how long to let it cook.
  7. Construct Tacos!
